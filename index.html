Investment Ladder
Built around your real timeline. The gold loan ending in 3 years is your defining financial event.
⚠️ This is educational guidance based on general financial principles — not professional financial advice. Consult a licensed financial advisor in Sri Lanka, especially about loan restructuring options.
NOW → MONTH 12 — Foundation
Micro Emergency Fund — LKR 120,000
Save LKR 10,000/month from HYB income into a separate account you never touch. Salary and tuition cover living costs and loans. HYB income builds the cushion. In 12 months you have LKR 120,000 — one month of loan payments covered if anything goes wrong.
LKR 10,000/month from HYB → LKR 120,000 in 12 months
MONTH 12–36 — Build while waiting for freedom
Grow Emergency Fund to LKR 300,000
As HYB income grows, increase the monthly savings amount. Target LKR 300,000 saved by the time the gold loan ends — this is your safety net and your investment seed capital combined. Keep it in a high-interest savings account (NSB or BOC Special Savings — 8–10%/year).
LKR 15,000–20,000/month → LKR 300,000+ by April 2029
APRIL 2029 — The Turning Point
Gold Loan Ends — LKR 150,000/month Freed
This single event is the biggest financial moment in your near future. The day it ends, you lock in this allocation immediately — before lifestyle creep can absorb it: LKR 50,000/month to Fixed Deposit (BOC or People's Bank, 9–12%/year), LKR 50,000/month to extra housing loan payment (cuts 6–8 years off 15-year term), LKR 30,000/month to TuitionHub.lk development fund, LKR 20,000/month to unit trust.
Total freed: LKR 150,000/month · None of it goes to lifestyle
POST APRIL 2029 — Accelerate
Fixed Deposit + Housing Prepayment + Unit Trust
With LKR 50,000/month going into a 12-month rolling FD you build LKR 600,000+/year in savings earning 9–12%. The extra housing payment reduces a 15-year term by 6–8 years, saving hundreds of thousands in interest. The unit trust (NDB Wealth or Softlogic Capital) at LKR 20,000/month grows at 8–14% annually.
FD: LKR 50k/mo · Housing prepay: LKR 50k/mo · Unit trust: LKR 20k/mo
WHEN HYB EARNS LKR 200,000+/MONTH
TuitionHub.lk — Your Highest-Return Investment
No FD or unit trust returns 600% per year. LKR 150,000 invested in building TuitionHub.lk, reaching 50 paying tutors at LKR 1,500/month, generates LKR 75,000/month recurring. That is the product. That is the end game. And you are the only person positioned to build it because you are already living the problem it solves.
Budget: LKR 150,000 one-time · Return: LKR 75,000/month at 50 tutors
YEAR 5+ — After housing loan is accelerated
CSE Blue Chip Stocks — Long-Term Wealth
Once the emergency fund is full, investments are running, and HYB is generating reliably — allocate 5% of monthly savings to 2–3 established CSE blue chip companies. Only money you can leave untouched for 3+ years. This is the final layer, not the first.
5% of savings only · Never invest what you cannot afford to leave for 3 years
